About

The Arab MedTechAI Organization was founded in 2020 in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Our mission is to build and deploy AI-powered medical systems that serve the Arab world — bridging the gap between advanced artificial intelligence and regional healthcare needs.

We develop intelligent clinical platforms that are culturally aligned, clinically reliable, and accessible across the region. From AI pharmacy tools to clinical decision support systems, our work empowers healthcare professionals with technology that enhances decision-making and improves patient outcomes.

With a growing suite of over 24 specialized AI tools and multiple platform deployments, we are committed to making advanced medical AI accessible to hospitals, pharmacies, and clinics throughout the Arab world.
