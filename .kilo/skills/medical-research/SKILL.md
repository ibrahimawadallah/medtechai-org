name: medical-research
description: Fetch current medical documentation, drug databases, and clinical guidelines
---
Use websearch and GitMCP to find up-to-date medical information:

1. For drug interactions: Search FDA, WHO, and clinical pharmacology sources
2. For dosing: Reference current guidelines (e.g., Sanford Guide, Lexicomp)
3. For ICD-10 codes: Use official WHO and CMS documentation
4. For medical APIs: Find Swagger/OpenAPI docs via GitMCP

Always provide publication dates and source URLs. Medical information older than 2 years should be flagged for verification.
