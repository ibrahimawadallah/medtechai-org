# Demo Call Preparation (Days 15-18)

## Demo Flow Script (5-10 minutes max)

### 1. Opening (30 seconds)
- Thank them for their time
- Confirm they're in a position to evaluate new tools for their pharmacy/clinic/hospital

### 2. Value Hook (1 minute)
- "Our users save 10+ hours weekly on medication research"
- "We catch 3-4 interaction alerts that would otherwise be missed"
- "All UAE Ministry of Health guidance is automatically included"

### 3. Interactive Demo (3 minutes)
Show the three key tools:
1. **Drug Search** (demo/interactive/drug-search.html)
   - Search for "Metformin" or "Atorvastatin"
   - Show mechanism of action, indications, warnings
   - Show side effects and pricing
   
2. **Interaction Checker** (/tools/interaction-checker/)
   - Demonstrate with common combination (Metformin + Atorvastatin)
   - Show severity levels and management recommendations
   
3. **rx-upload Medical Document Analyzer** (/tools/rx-upload/)
   - Show dashboard layout
   - Highlight comprehensive analysis capabilities

### 4. Pain Point Discovery (2 minutes)
Ask ONE question at a time, LISTEN:

- "What's your biggest challenge with current drug reference tools?"
- "How do you and your team typically check for drug interactions today?"
- "What would saving 10+ hours weekly mean for your operations?"

### 5. Next Steps (30 seconds)
- "Would you like to try the free plan with your team?"
- "Or should we schedule a deeper dive with your purchasing/procurement team?"
