# MedTechAI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

MedTechAI mirrors the Wolters Kluwer About Us page structure and design language — professional, trustworthy, and impact-driven.

### Brand Colors
- **Teal Primary**: `#38B8AE` — CTAs, highlights, accent elements
- **Deep Navy**: `#1A2D3A` — Headlines, body text
- **Mint Mist**: `#E6F7F6` — Backgrounds, card tints, badges
- **Soft Teal**: `#D1EFED` — Dividers, borders, subtle accents
- **Slate Gray**: `#6B8A8E` — Secondary text, captions, muted labels
- **Pure White**: `#FFFFFF` — Surfaces, contrast backgrounds

### Typography
- **Headlines**: DM Sans (700/600 weight), 26–40px
- **Body**: Inter (400 weight), 13–16px
- **Captions**: Inter (500–600 weight), 11–12px
- **Stats/Numbers**: DM Sans (700 weight), 32–36px

## About Us Page Structure (WK-style)

1. **Hero** — "Deep impact when it matters most" with CTA
2. **Stats bar** — 4-column grid with key metrics (teal divider lines)
3. **Our Organization** — Divisions grid (2-col) with icon, name, desc, reach, product tags
4. **Customer Portfolio** — 4-column percentage stats
5. **What Makes Us Different** — 2x2 grid with icon, heading, body, link
6. **Connected by Values** — 4-column values grid
7. **Principles** — 3-column cards with teal left border
8. **CTA Banner** — Full-width gradient teal banner with white button

## Design Patterns

- **Section labels**: Teal line + uppercase teal text (11px, 1.5px letter-spacing)
- **Cards**: White bg, `#D1EFED` border, 12–16px radius, hover shadow
- **CTA buttons**: Teal bg, white text, 12px radius, DM Sans
- **Footer**: Deep Navy bg, white text at 60% opacity
- **Hero CTA**: `&rarr;` arrow, teal bg, white text

## Content Rules

- Always lead with "Deep impact when it matters most"
- Use `&rarr;` for CTA arrows
- Use `&mdash;` for em dashes
- Keep section labels uppercase with tracking
- Stats use "50+" format (no decimal)
- Division product tags: mint bg, navy text, 11px
- AI tools describe clinical value first, technology second
